Running Stuck In Time

Running Stuck in Time is a browser game exploring the different bureaucratic obstacles placed throughout the migrant’s journey. Regardless of how one migrates to the Netherlands, they will face with certain requirements, changing policies, and hidden expectations. Some forms of migrating is easier than others. The experiences, although commonly shared, are all unique based on the persons particular situation.
The game is here to place you running stuck in time, perpetually waiting, ever-late and hopefully one day, legally belonging.
The story will take you through the requirements. During the different levels make sure to pay attention to your application health, wealth and joy stats, you can still win a visa if you are miserable but you do need the money to survive. If your application health is below a certain point you lose the game.
