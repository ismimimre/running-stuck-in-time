Running Stuck In Time

Running Stuck in Time is a browser game exploring the different bureaucratic obstacles placed throughout the migrant’s journey. Regardless of how one migrates to the Netherlands, they will face with certain requirements, changing policies, and hidden expectations. Some forms of migrating is easier than others. The experiences, although commonly shared, are all unique based on the persons particular situation. It is easer to move with a company’s sponsorship, it is relatively straightforward to stay with a Highly Skilled Migrant Visa, it is mildly frustrating to remain as an artist, and practically freezing to be an asylum seeker. More than anything it depends on the money in your pockets and how desirable your previous citizenship is the the Dutch Government.

This game hopes to offer different routes of migration as various levels representing actual peoples experiences to offer you, the player, a window into this maze of bureaucracy. You could be intimately familiar with the levels, or this game could merely offer a brief introduction. I am trying to either gamify you anxiety inducing experiences, or to frustrate you into relating to migrant struggles. In both cases, the game is here to place you running stuck in time, perpetually waiting, ever-late and hopefully one day, legally belonging.

For our Projects in Progress demonstration, I am offering you three levels from the Artist Visa route. Play, win or fail them.

I desperately need your feedback, I need to know how the games are experienced to improve and adjust them. So please fill out the Feedback forms, place them in blue envelops and further traumatize me.
